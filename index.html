About

Letterman and Moore Properties LLC exists to obtain, manage, lease, and sell real estate as well as manage other real estate investments in order to generate returns to its investors.

  • 2019

    Humble Beginnings

    The idea for Letterman and Moore Properties originally came from Charles Oehmke and Matthew Merrill, recent alumni of the University of California, Los Angeles. Together, along with six other UCLA students, they formed Letterman and Moore Properties on August 3, 2019.
